Safety

"Safety isn’t expensive, it’s priceless "

We here in Marine Mechanics understand the value and seriousness of Marine Safety Equipment. We are a complete safety solution provider – Everything you need your vessel’s to remain safe and complaint.

The MSC requirements and IMO guidelines are constantly being reviewed and upgraded depending on the situations and events that are being experienced by the Maritime fraternity at sea. LSA and FFA code is considered to be the most important aspect of anyvessel’s safety requirements be it to be its men or materials. There are several rules and regulations framed by the flag states that are inline with the MSC circulars and guidelines to safeguard mariner’s life, environment and materials onboard.

MM Safety has made an effort to harmonize these varying service needs on all the LSA and FFA items, and Our services will cover all the Annual, Biennial, 3 yearly, 5 yearly, 10 yearly service requirements as per the flag state and className requirements.
